Privacy Policy

Pocket Flow is a personal budgeting and expense tracking app. We value your privacy and keep your data on-device.

Overview

Collection & Storage

Pocket Flow stores expense-related data locally on your device. The app does not collect, upload, or transmit personal or financial data to external servers.

The following information is only stored locally if you add it:

  • Expense amounts and categories
  • Dates and times
  • Notes
  • Imported files (for example, CSV backups from other budgeting apps)

Data Sharing & Usage

Data Sharing

Third parties

Pocket Flow does not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data outside your device.

Data Usage

In-app only

Your data is used solely within the app for tasks such as:

  • Displaying expense summaries
  • Storing, processing, importing, and exporting entries
  • Generating charts, insights, and analytics

Pocket Flow does not use your data for advertising or tracking.

Retention & Security

Data Retention & Deletion

All data stays on your device until you remove it. You can delete data by:

  • Removing individual expense entries
  • Clearing all app data from Android system settings
  • Deleting imported files from your device storage

No copies are kept by Pocket Flow.

Security

On-device safeguards

The app relies on Android's built-in sandboxing and secure storage. Pocket Flow does not transmit data over the internet unless you manually share an exported file.

Additional Notes

File Imports & Exports

Imported data (like CSV backups) is processed on-device only. Exported files are saved to your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

Pocket Flow is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Updates will appear on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
